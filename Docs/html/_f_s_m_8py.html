Description:

This node manages the overall behaviour of the robot by implementing a Finite States Machine: it is made of 4 different states (LoadOntology, Decide, Surveillance, Recharging), that are reached through 5 different transitions ('loaded','decided','visited','low_battery','recharged') and within which it makes us of the functions defined in the Helper class contained in 'helper.py' script.
